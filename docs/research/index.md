# Research

Academic foundations and ongoing research behind Noetic Eidos.

## Philosophy

> **Breaking down mathematical gatekeeping.**
> 
> Advanced mathematical concepts shouldn't be hidden behind jargon and inaccessible papers. We believe in making sophisticated ideas accessible while maintaining theoretical rigor.

## Core Papers

### Information Geometry

- Amari, S. (1985). *Differential-Geometrical Methods in Statistics*
- Čencov, N. N. (1982). *Statistical Decision Rules and Optimal Inference*
- Ay, N. et al. (2017). *Information Geometry*

### Zeta Functions

- Riemann, B. (1859). *On the Number of Primes Less Than a Given Magnitude*
- Edwards, H. M. (2001). *Riemann's Zeta Function*
- Titchmarsh, E. C. (1986). *The Theory of the Riemann Zeta-Function*

### Attention Mechanisms

- Vaswani, A. et al. (2017). *Attention Is All You Need*
- Choromanski, K. et al. (2021). *Rethinking Attention with Performers*
